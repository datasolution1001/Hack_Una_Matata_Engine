How it Works

This engine give each team member a score, and then the scores of the same team members will be added to each other.

Priority

  1. Participants who present in one of Rayen Falah's Sessions they have 5 points and for each another session they attend we add 1 point to their score
  2. Participants who did not participate in RF Session, but they are members of GDSC ISI they have 4 points added to the score fo the team
  3. Participants who are not from ISI Ariana but they are from another GDSC they take 3 points added to the score of the team
  4. Participants from another university or from isi they take 2 points added to the score of the team
N.b: all the participants of the team must be beginners, if one of them is not a beginner all team members will be refused
N.b: all participants to be put in the system must be beginner
