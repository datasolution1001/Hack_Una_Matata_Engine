How it Works

This tool rates each team member and adds up their scores to get the team's total score.

Priority

  1. Attendees earn 5 points for presenting in one of Rayen Falah's sessions, and for each additional session they attend, we add 1 point to their score.
  2. Participants who are members of GDSC ISI but did not participate in Rayen Falah's (RF) session receive 4 points added to the team's score.
  3. Participants from a different GDSC (not ISI Ariana) receive an additional 3 points added to the team's score.
  4. Participants from another university or ISI receive an extra 2 points added to the team's score.
N.b: All team members must be beginners; if any participant is not a beginner, the entire team will be disqualified.
N.b: To be included in the system, all participants must be beginners.
